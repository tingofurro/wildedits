Welcome to WildEdits!
This is a research demo of the paper: Beyond the Chat: Executable and Verifiable Text-Editing with LLMs. Here's a 2-min video introducing WildEdits's features:
To get started, click on "+ New Document" to create a new document.
Data Retention
Since this is a research demo, we are logging data and interaction so we can analyze how people use WildEdits, and understand how to improve it.
If you would a document to be excluded from any analysis (and all information regarding that document deleted), you can click on the trashcan "delete" icon next to the document name. This will permanently delete the document, and all associated data.
